ABOUT TAS

THE GROWING THREAT OF SYNTHETIC MEDIA

The ability to manipulate media is not new, but the landscape has changed dramatically in recent years. According to reports from multiple security agencies, the scale in use of media manipulation has dramatically increased as the complexity of leveraging manipulated media has fallen.

Making sophisticated fakes previously could take professionals days to weeks, but now these fakes can be produced in a fraction of the time with limited or no technical expertise.

Real-World Harms

  • • Financial Fraud: Deepfake video call scams have resulted in millions of dollars in losses.
  • • Market Manipulation: Fake images shared on social media have affected financial markets.
  • • Political Disinformation: Deepfakes of political figures cause confusion and undermine trust.

TECHNICAL ARCHITECTURE

SECURE BY DESIGN

Cryptographic Security

TAS leverages robust RSA public-private key infrastructure to ensure only authorized users can create valid signatures.

Dynamic Authentication

For videos, TAS signatures dynamically update every three seconds with new timestamps, making it virtually impossible for malicious actors to fake the authentication chain.

For Images:

A static TAS signature is embedded into the image. All four QR codes contain fixed information based on the moment of creation.

For Videos:

The TAS signature dynamically updates every three seconds with a new timestamp. Each update changes all four QR codes, as the timestamp is included in the hash sum of every code.
