HOW IT WORKS

THE FOUR PILLARS OF TAS AUTHENTICATION

TAS is a visible signature that you can apply to your content, and it lets anyone who scans it know that they are seeing your True Authentic Self. The signature is made up of four QR codes in the shape of a T that encode four vitally important pieces of information about the content being signed.

Identity (Who?)

Authenticates that the creator is who they claim to be.

Content (What?)

Ties the verification to the specific content to prevent misuse.

Location (Where?)

Verifies the geographic location where the content was created.

Time (When?)

Confirms when the content was produced with secure timestamps.

TECHNICAL ARCHITECTURE

SECURE BY DESIGN

Cryptographic Security

TAS leverages the robust RSA cryptosystem to ensure only you can create your own valid signatures.

Dynamic Authentication

For videos, TAS signatures dynamically update every three seconds with new timestamps, making it virtually impossible for malicious actors to fake the authentication chain.

For Images:

A static TAS signature is embedded into the image. All four QR codes contain fixed information based on the moment of creation.

For Videos:

The TAS signature dynamically updates every three seconds, like the 30 second updates of authenticator apps. Each update changes all four QR codes adding exponential security.
