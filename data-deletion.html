Data Deletion Instructions

If you connected your Facebook or Instagram account to TAS and would like to delete the data associated with it, follow these steps:

  1. Open the TAS app and go to your Profile
  2. Tap the settings icon (⚙) in the top-right corner
  3. Scroll to the bottom and tap Delete Account
  4. Confirm deletion when prompted

Deleting your account permanently removes all personal data TAS holds about you, including any information obtained through your Facebook or Instagram connection. Content records you created are retained, but they contain only a anonymous identifier and metadata about when and where the content was created — no personal information.

This action cannot be undone.

No longer have access to the app?
Email support@scan-tas.com and we will delete your data manually within 30 days.
